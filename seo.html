SEO Plans, Explained

Which level of SEO is right for you?

Three levels, three jobs. Here's the difference in plain English, and which plan each one lives on.

🔧

Basic SEO Setup

Starter plan · The job: get found.

A one-time technical foundation so Google can crawl, index, and correctly understand your site: title tags and meta descriptions on every page, LocalBusiness schema, an XML sitemap, Google Search Console and analytics connected, plus fast, mobile-friendly pages. You show up for your business name and the obvious searches. Foundational, not a growth engine.

📈

On-Page SEO + Blog

Professional plan · The job: get ranked.

Ongoing, keyword-driven work: keyword and competitor research, optimizing each page around your target "service + city" terms, dedicated location pages, Google Business Profile management, and 1 to 2 blog posts a month. This is how you actually climb for the searches that bring in paying customers, and it improves month over month.

🚀

Custom SEO Strategy

Business plan · The job: dominate.

A full custom strategy and execution: aggressive content, multi-location targeting, link building, advanced reporting, and a dedicated account manager. For businesses that want to own their market and outrank everyone in it.

Rule of thumb: Basic makes your site correct. On-page makes it competitive. Custom makes it dominant. Most local businesses that want to win the Google map pack start on the Professional plan.
