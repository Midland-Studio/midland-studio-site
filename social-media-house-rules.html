How We Work Together

A Few House Rules

These keep your content on time, on brand, and stress-free. They spell out exactly who handles what, so nothing gets missed.

1

Assets & Deadlines

All photos, videos, and brand assets must be uploaded to your designated Google Drive folder by the 20th of the month before posting. We can't build or schedule posts without them — if assets arrive late, your posts for the upcoming month may be delayed or missed.

2

Content Approval

Once your monthly content calendar is ready, you'll have 3 business days to review and request edits. If we don't hear back within 3 business days, posts are scheduled as-is so we never miss your posting window.

3

Community Management Please Note

These packages cover content creation and scheduling. They do not include replying to comments, DMs, or customer-service inquiries — you remain the direct voice of your community. Want us to handle it? Ask about our community-management add-on.

4

Billing & Cancellation

Services are billed monthly, in advance. We ask for 30 days' written notice to cancel or pause, so we can wrap up any scheduled queues cleanly.
